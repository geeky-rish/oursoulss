Our Services

PROFESSIONAL THERAPY

Let's lighten your load together. Are you ready?

A professional therapist supports you in creating strategies to overcome personal challenges, including mental health issues like depression, stress, and anxiety, among others. Our therapists are here to guide you through these challenges, helping you navigate life's difficulties. Let's collaborate to ease your burdens and uplift your spirits.

  • 50 Minute session with a professional Therapist
  • Implement personal strategies and advice
  • Discuss your concerns openly and safely
  • Receive insights with renewed professional perspectives

Starting @1,699/month

NON-PROFESSIONAL THERAPY

Looking to make your journey a little easier?

A non-professional therapist can provide a supportive and understanding presence, helping you navigate and overcome your challenges while ensuring you feel acknowledged, understood, and free from judgment, providing empathy to help you feel better.

  • 50 Minute session with a non-professional Therapist
  • Use this session as a safe space to vent and discuss your problems
  • Complete Therapist provided tasks and activities
  • We listen to you throughout the process

Starting @599/month
